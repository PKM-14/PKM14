About Me (Professional)

I am Pranit Malhotra, a student at St Olave's Grammar School in Orpington. I have a strong academic interest in Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry, especially within the realm of Aerodynamics and Aerospace Engineering.

I have participated in prestigious competitions such as the UKMT Maths Challenge, advancing to both the Junior and Intermediate Olympiads, and I placed in the Top 100 in the British Physics Olympiad.

As a member of Team Blitz in the F1 in Schools competition (now STEM Racing), I discovered a passion for precision engineering and manufacturing. This project allowed me to develop teamwork, leadership, and resilience while striving for engineering excellence.

My leadership extends beyond academics—I captained the U13 to U15 Rugby B teams and later joined the U15 A team. These experiences taught me to inspire others, unite teams, and lead both on and off the pitch.

About Me (Personal)

My passion lies deeply in the world of Physics, where I am fascinated by the way the universe works, from the smallest particles to the vastness of space. I have always been curious about the fundamental forces that govern our reality, and this curiosity has led me to dive into areas like Aerodynamics and Aerospace Engineering. I love the challenge of solving complex problems and the excitement that comes with understanding the intricate laws of nature. Whether it’s through my studies or my hands-on experiences, I am constantly driven by a desire to learn and apply scientific concepts in real-world scenarios. This passion for physics fuels my ambition to pursue a career in aerospace, where I hope to contribute to groundbreaking discoveries and innovations that push the boundaries of human exploration.

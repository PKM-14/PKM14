About Me

I am Pranit Malhotra, a highly motivated student at St Olave's Grammar School with a strong academic focus in Physics, Mathematics, and Aerospace Engineering. My achievements include competing in the UKMT Junior and Intermediate Olympiads, ranking in the Top 100 nationally in the British Physics Olympiad, and developing a deep understanding of aerodynamics and experimental design through STEM Racing (F1 in Schools).

My work on the Arkwright Portable Wind Tunnel project and STEM Racing D&E portfolio reflects my commitment to precision, iterative prototyping, and leadership in team-based engineering projects. I aim to push the boundaries of aerospace engineering while nurturing curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving skills in all my work.
